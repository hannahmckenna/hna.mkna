"OPEN WIDE"

A project centring around process.

I came across old E-1 Slides of images my grandad had taken throughout his dental career.

Finding these slides inspired me to recreate the memories they contained by going through the same processes he did while capturing these images.

When recreating his process I used the same film stock my grandad did in the 1970s.

I took photos of teeth on the very same camera he used and I tried my best to emulate what I imagined his process would have been.

While looking at the images I fund that the models poses naturally mirrored that of my grandads patients.

I had unintentionally captured images that directly correlated to his through following his process.
