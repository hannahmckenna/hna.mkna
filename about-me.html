ABOUT ME

I am currently studying as an incoming Final year Graphic Design student in The National College of Art and Design (NCAD).

My favourite projects are always those that start at one point and finish somewhere completely different.

The developmental process is something that I thoroughly enjoy. Having the ability to think through an idea and it to its conclusion is something that I love and is something that excites me. Determination and work ethic gave me the ability to continue to explore and push an idea to fruition.

The main focus of my work, as of recent has been photography based. Using both analogue and digital photography I find that I can best communicate my concepts and ideas through this medium. I have also been working on typographic work and attempting to find styles and techniques that best suit me and my work.

My current goal for this upcoming year is to find an Internship in which I can grow and learn from those in a career that I wish to pursue. I hope to be in an environment that I can contribute to, gain experience in and meet people I can learn from.
